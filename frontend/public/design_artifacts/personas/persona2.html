Persona 2: Linda

Linda, age 45, is a resident nurse in an intensive care unit of a hospital in Cincinnati, Ohio who lives with her husband, and two teenage sons in Cincinnati. She has her bachelor's degree in nursing from the University of Cincinnati and is a certified resident nurse. Her elderly mother, Dorothy lives nearby and has recently been diagnosed with hypertension, high cholesterol, and early-stage diabetes. Linda spends a significant amount of time each day managing her mother’s medications, meal planning, and ensuring her mother stays active.

Linda wants to find a way to remotely monitor her mother’s health metrics, such as blood pressure, weight, and activity level, without having to physically be there. She wants to reduce the potential risk of health complications by making sure her mother adheres to her medication regiment and a heart-healthy diet via regular health check-ins.

Linda struggles with balancing her responsibilities as a nurse, a mother, and a caregiver. She needs an app that can provide real-time health data about her mother, share alerts for critical changes, and simplify the tracking of health metrics like blood pressure and dietary intake. She is also concerned about setting reminders for her mother in a way that is easy to follow.

Linda is comfortable using health and fitness apps on her phone but needs an intuitive, elder-friendly interface for her mother. She’s also looking for an app with secure sharing features so that both she and her mother’s doctor can stay informed.
