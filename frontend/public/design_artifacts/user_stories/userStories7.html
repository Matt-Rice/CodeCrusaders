User Stories 7: Olivia

Macro Tracking and Meal Prep Management

  • As a busy professional, Olivia wants to log her meals quickly so that she can stay on track with her nutrition goals without taking too much time out of her day.
  • As a fitness enthusiast, Olivia wants an app that tracks her macronutrient intake and provides a visual breakdown of her daily protein, carb, and fat intake so she can easily monitor her progress.
  • As someone balancing work and fitness, Olivia wants the app to suggest easy-to-prepare recipes that align with her dietary goals so she can maintain a balanced diet without added stress.
  • As someone training for a half-marathon, Olivia wants to see her macronutrient intake over the week in a simple visual format so she can assess her progress and make adjustments as needed.
  • As a busy individual, Olivia wants the app to provide tips for prepping meals in advance so that she can stay consistent with her diet even during hectic workdays.
  • As a health-conscious eater, Olivia wants to filter recipes based on her specific macro goals so she can quickly find meals that meet her nutritional needs.
  • As someone focused on fitness, Olivia wants reminders to meet her daily macro targets, especially protein, so she can ensure her diet supports her training.
  • As a tech-savvy but busy professional, Olivia wants an intuitive and simple interface for tracking her meals and macros so she can stay consistent without feeling overwhelmed by complex features.
