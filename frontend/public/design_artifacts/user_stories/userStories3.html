User Stories 3: James

Weight, Blood Pressure, and Exercise Management

  • As a retired person managing heart health, James needs to log his daily weight so that he can monitor changes and manage his fitness.
  • As a hypertension patient, James needs to log his daily blood pressure readings so that he and his doctor can track his heart health and note any changes.
  • As a fitness beginner, James wants to log his exercise so that he can track his physical activity and be motivated to stay active.
  • As a novice health app user, James needs a simple and clear interface for logging health data so that he can feel comfortable using the app.
  • As a heart patient, James wants to see visual graphs and trends of his health metrics over time so that he can view his progress and evaluate the efficacy of lifestyle changes.
  • As a senior, James wants to be able to set daily reminders to log his weight and blood pressure at the same times each day to make sure the results are consistent and so that he does not forget to log them.
  • As a father and heart patient, James wants to share his health data with his sons and his doctor so they can get an idea about his well-being and make changes if needed.
  • As a heart patient living alone, James needs to receive urgent notifications if there is a significant change in his condition so that he can know if he needs to make urgent changes or call someone.
  • As a heart patient living alone, James needs an app that can notify his doctor and his sons about urgent changes in his medical history so that they can help him to take the appropriate actions.
