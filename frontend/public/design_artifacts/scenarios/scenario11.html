Scenario 11

Health Monitoring

Bob, has always had health concerns having a autoimmune disorder growing up. he is currently attenting collage at the university of louisville. in his dorm he has one roommate. after moving into his dorm he has had some problems arise due to his autoimmune disorder and his doctor has perscribed him to follow new treatment plans

Bob, Bob has grown up with lots of medications his whole life but now that he has left the nest and gone to collage he now will be in charge of his own health. he needs a app that can track when and what mediecnes to take it being improtant that he can have a good system that allows him to know what bottles to take his pills from there being many it being confusing.

Bob, wants to find a app that will help him autoatically remind him and give him a picture that shows what bottle and what amounts of pills he needs and when he needs them.

Bob hopes by using our app he can set up his medcation schedule once and be able to not worry about it with his busy schedule of school. using the app he can now see what medications he needs to take and when he needs to take them.
