Scenario 6

Health Metric Reminders

Linda, a registered nurse in Cincinnati, lives with her husband and two teenage sons. Her elderly mother, Dorothy, recently had a health scare when her blood pressure spiked dangerously high, leading to a brief hospitalization. After this incident, Dorothy’s doctor emphasized the importance of regularly monitoring her blood pressure and other health metrics to manage her hypertension, high cholesterol, and early-stage diabetes. If her blood pressure or glucose levels become unstable, her health could rapidly decline, which worries Linda.

Dorothy has never been consistent with her health routines and finds it challenging to remember to check her blood pressure and blood sugar levels daily. She’s used to taking medication when she remembers and hasn’t needed to track her health metrics as precisely before. Now, however, Dorothy’s doctor has asked her to check her blood pressure twice daily—once in the morning and once at night—and to monitor her blood sugar levels every afternoon. Missing these check-ins could lead to serious health risks, and Linda is determined to help her mother establish a reliable routine.

Linda has tried setting alarms on Dorothy’s phone to remind her to take these measurements, but Dorothy often dismisses them without following through, as they don’t provide detailed guidance or support. Linda wants an app that can set persistent reminders for her mother’s health checks that won’t stop until Dorothy confirms each task. She also needs a feature where Dorothy can easily enter her blood pressure and blood sugar readings, with large buttons and a straightforward design suited for her mother’s needs.

Additionally, Linda wants to be notified once Dorothy completes her measurements each day so she can keep track of her mother’s health from afar. If Dorothy forgets or skips a check-in, Linda wants the app to alert her, allowing her to follow up with a call or visit. She also hopes to share Dorothy’s readings directly with her doctor to make adjustments in real time if needed. With this app, Linda envisions a solution that provides peace of mind for both her and her mother. Dorothy would receive clear, gentle reminders that guide her through her daily health checks, and Linda could stay informed about her mother’s health without needing to be there every moment. This way, Dorothy could retain her independence, while Linda ensures her mother’s safety and well-being.
