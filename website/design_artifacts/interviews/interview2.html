Interview 2

Interview Date: 10/26/24
Interviewer: Matt Rice

Background

  • Mathematics student and a tutor for high school and university students specializing in mathematics.

Summary

  • Yes, I would use this app because I have dietary restrictions and need to monitor what I eat for health reasons.
  • I would also use it because I take medicine several times a day and sometimes it can be difficult to keep track of.
  • This would prove useful if I was looking after someone especially if I am taking care of that person with someone else as it makes it easier to keep track of their medicine and it can let the other person if a medicine has been administered to avoid double doses.

Needs

  • Be able to customize dietary restrictions to adhere to different cultural requirements, allergy restrictions to make sure that users do not consume anything that is harmful for them.
  • Keep in mind a budget for how much food might cost as it might be difficult for certain people to manage the price of foods while also trying to meet their goals.
  • Should be on Android or IOS so that it can be accessed easily by users and more convenient to use.

Ideas

  • When you take medicine click a button and it can send notifications to people of your choice.
  • For price point, it could be free, or a low price, or be free but require a doctorâ€™s recommendation.
  • Potentially doctors could be able to view your medicine and diet.

Concerns

  • If there is personal information shared regarding health, a data breach could be very bad.
  • Suggested meal plans need to be dietician approved.
