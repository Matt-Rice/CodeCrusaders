Scenario 13

Meal Search and Tracking

Adam, feeling stress over his job, wants to feel more energized by focusing more on his health. He figured the best way to start is by watching what he eats. Adam isn’t sure what foods will help him reduce his stress, or how to accurately track the foods he eats.

Adam tries looking up meals online, but with too much information and overwhelming text, he loses track of what he’s trying to do. All the new unorganized information only adds to his stress. Adam is also new to nutrient tracking and finds it difficult to track his intake without all the nutritional information on the food recipe he looked up.

Adam is looking for an app that not only looks up recipes for him, recipes that would help with Adams stress and to help energize his health. It would also sort the recipes that are quick and easy to make, helping Adam quickly get back to his business. The nutritional values for the recipes will also be available to view, and would automatically log his meals. This would help Adam keep track of his health easier and see if hes reaching his goal of becoming healthier.
