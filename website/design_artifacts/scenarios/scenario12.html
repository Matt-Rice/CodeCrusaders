Scenario 12

Elder Health Care

Bob, is a current collage student . he is living in the dorms with one roomate during his time studying. recently now that he has moved out and is no longer home his elderly grandmother he lived with no longer has him to rely on for help with her medication.

Bob, has expericne using apps but needs a app that he can teach and trust his grandmother with to help her with her medications. she is somewhat familar with how to use her phone but too many interfaces and anything complicated confuses her and can lead to issues.

Bob, beeds a app that is easily to navigate even for someone unfamilar with mobile applications. he needs a app that can tell his grandmother what medication to take and when to take it so he doesnt have to worry about he missing dosages while he is away at collage. he needs a reminder that will show up and pop up and alert her loudly then allowing her to click on the notification then it brings her to a image of her medication/s and how much she would need to take now

By setting up this app for his grandmother he hopes she will be able to take her correct medicaion at correct times to hopefully avoid overdosage or incorrect medcations which could lead to much worse medical porblems.
