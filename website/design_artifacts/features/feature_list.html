Feature List

Feature: Medicine Reminders
Description:
  • As a person that must take medicine at specific times of the day, James needs to be able to set reminders to take his medicine at specific times so that he takes his medicine at the same times every day.
  • As a tech-savvy senior, James needs persistent alerts on his phone to make sure he does not miss any reminders.
  • As a new taker of medicine, James needs reminders that will not go away until he confirms that he has taken his medicine so that he is less likely to miss a dose.
  • As a person who must take pills in the morning when he might be asleep, James wants to be able to customize alarm sounds and settings so that he does not miss a medication due to a quiet alarm.
  • As a person who must take several medicines, James needs to be able to write detailed information about each of his pills so that he can make sure that he knows what the pill looks like, and how much he needs to take.
  • As a father who lives alone, James wants an app that is able to send a confirmation message that he took his medicine to his sons so that his sons can stay informed about his medicine intake.
Constraints:
  • The user interface needs to be simple and easy to use as all of the information that is needed can be quite complex.
  • This would likely have to be on a mobile environment for convenience, and people are more likely to use alarms on their phones.
Comments:
  • Via James's user stories
Feature: Daily Nutrient goals and meal logging for different diet types
Description:
  • As a person who wants to eat healthier, Adam needs recommended meal plans.
  • As a person who is busy and usually skips meals, Adam wants to be reminded when it’s time to eat.
  • As a person under stress, Adam wants recipes that take minimal effort as not to add to his stress.
  • As a DASH diet beginner, James wants to search for DASH-compliant recipes so that he can find heart-healthy meals that fit his dietary restrictions.
  • As a person who has never used a meal planning app, James needs an intuitive, easy to understand user interface so that he can feel comfortable and not feel overwhelmed when using the app.
  • As a person starting a new diet, James needs clear images and easy and understandable steps to follow for each recipe so that he can confidently prepare meals that align with his health goals.
  • As a nutrition tracker, James wants to view a list of recently logged meals so that it will be quicker to log them in the future and to make sure he doesn’t log a meal twice.
  • As a dietary novice, James wants an organized database of DASH recipes so that he can find meals he likes without searching extensively.
Constraints:
  • Diet plans would need to be approved by a professional otherwise they could be unsafe.
  • We would likely have to web scrape for open-source recipes because none of us are chefs.
Comments:
  • Via James's and Adam's user stories
Feature: Data Sharing with Doctors and People of User’s Choice
Description:
  • As a father who lives alone, James wants an app that is able to send a confirmation message that he took his medicine to his sons so that his sons can stay informed about his medicine intake.
  • As a father, James wants to share his weekly food logs with his sons and his doctor so that they can monitor his diet and stay updated on his progress.
  • As a father and heart patient, James wants to share his health data with his sons and his doctor so they can get an idea about his well-being and make changes if needed.
Constraints:
  • Some people might be tempted to turn off notifications because they could prove to be annoying. So probably try to not overdo the number of notifications sent.
Comments:
  • Via James's user stories
Feature: Logging Blood Pressure, Weight, and Exercise
Description:
  • As a retired person managing heart health, James needs to log his daily weight so that he can monitor changes and manage his fitness.
  • As a hypertension patient, James needs to log his daily blood pressure readings so that he and his doctor can track his heart health and note any changes.
  • As a fitness beginner, James wants to log his exercise so that he can track his physical activity and be motivated to stay active.
  • As a novice health app user, James needs a simple and clear interface for logging health data so that he can feel comfortable using the app.
  • As a senior, James wants to be able to set daily reminders to log his weight and blood pressure at the same times each day to make sure the results are consistent and so that he does not forget to log them.
  • As a heart patient, James wants to see visual graphs and trends of his health metrics over time so that he can view his progress and evaluate the efficacy of lifestyle changes.
Constraints:
  • Might need to include some instructions on how to properly take blood pressure and weight so that the results are consistent and accurate.
  • Finding a way to tie exercise into health monitoring could be difficult.
Comments:
  • Via James's user stories
Feature: Urgent Notifications about Health Changes
Description:
  • As a heart patient living alone, James needs an app that can notify his doctor and his sons about urgent changes in his medical history so that they can help him to take the appropriate actions.
  • As a heart patient living alone, James needs to receive urgent notifications if there is a significant change in his condition so that he can know if he needs to make urgent changes or call someone.
  • As a father who lives alone, James wants an app that is able to send a confirmation message that he took his medicine to his sons so that his sons can stay informed about his medicine intake.
Constraints:
  • Urgent reminders would be based off of if a dose or doses has/have been missed or if blood pressure has spiked to unsafe levels. As far as I know there is not a one hundred percent reliable way to see if you have AFib, but if that is available in the future, that could be included.
Comments:
  • Via James's user stories
