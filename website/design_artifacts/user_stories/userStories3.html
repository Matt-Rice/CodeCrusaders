User Stories 3

Scenario 3: Weight, Blood Pressure, and Exercise Management

James, who is recently retired and living alone with two sons nearby, has started the DASH diet and medication regimen to manage his high blood pressure and cholesterol. His doctor recommended not only dietary adjustments but also tracking his weight, blood pressure, and exercise regularly to monitor his heart health and overall fitness. James wants a simple, accessible way to log these metrics, as his doctor emphasized that regular logging could help detect any changes early, reducing his risk of further complications.

James has never tracked health data on his phone before, so he’s looking for a straightforward app that won’t overwhelm him. The app should allow him to log daily weight, blood pressure, and exercise in an easy-to-navigate format. It should also let him view trends in his data over time, so he can see his progress and stay motivated, set reminders for taking weight and blood pressure at the same time every day. It should also be able to share his data with his sons and his doctor, allowing them to get an idea of his health and notify them of any significant changes.

By using this app, James aims to stay accountable to his health goals, improve his heart health, and keep his family and healthcare providers informed about his well-being, ultimately supporting a healthier, more active lifestyle.
