User Stories 2: James

DASH Diet Monitoring

  1. As a DASH diet beginner, James wants to search for DASH-compliant recipes so that he can find heart-healthy meals that fit his dietary restrictions.
  2. As a retired accountant, James wants a simple, visual indicator of his nutrient limits so that he can quickly and easily see if he is close to exceeding his daily nutrient goals.
  3. As a patient with heart problems, James needs to be able to log his daily fat and sodium intake so that he can track his diet and reduce his risk of heart complications.
  4. As a person who has never used a meal planning app, James needs an intuitive, easy-to-understand user interface so that he can feel comfortable and not feel overwhelmed when using the app.
  5. As a father, James wants to share his weekly food logs with his sons and his doctor so that they can monitor his diet and stay updated on his progress.
  6. As a nutrition tracker, James wants to view a list of recently logged meals so that it will be quicker to log them in the future and to make sure he doesnâ€™t log a meal twice.
  7. As a person starting a new diet, James needs clear images and easy-to-understand steps for each recipe so that he can confidently prepare meals that align with his health goals.
  8. As a dietary novice, James wants an organized database of DASH recipes so that he can find meals he likes without searching extensively.
