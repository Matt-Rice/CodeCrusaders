User Stories 6: Linda

Health Metric Reminders

  • As a daughter and caregiver, Linda wants to set persistent reminders for her mother to take her blood pressure and check her blood sugar at specific times each day so that her mother can stay consistent with her health routine.
  • As a daughter, Linda wants her mother to receive easy-to-follow notifications that won’t stop until she confirms she has completed each check-in, ensuring Dorothy remembers her daily health tasks.
  • As a caregiver, Linda wants her mother to have a simple interface to enter her blood pressure and blood sugar readings so that Dorothy can easily log her metrics without confusion.
  • As a busy nurse, Linda wants to receive notifications when her mother completes her health check-ins each day so she can stay updated on her mother’s health without being physically present.
  • As a caregiver managing her mother’s health, Linda wants the app to alert her if Dorothy misses a check-in, so she can quickly remind her mother to take her readings.
  • As a daughter, Linda wants to securely share her mother’s blood pressure and blood sugar readings with Dorothy’s doctor so that her mother’s care team has access to her daily metrics.
  • As a nurse, Linda wants to monitor trends in her mother’s health metrics over time so she can identify any concerning patterns and address potential health issues early.
  • As a caregiver, Linda wants to customize Dorothy’s reminders with voice prompts or easy-to-read text, ensuring that her mother finds the app straightforward and accessible.
