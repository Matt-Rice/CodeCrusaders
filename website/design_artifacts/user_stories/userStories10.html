User Stories 10: Bob

Scenario 10: Diet Management

  1. As a person who wants to set up a diet plan, Bob needs to be able to set up reminnders about when and what meals he is able to eat and how to make them.
  2. As a person who is trying to diet he needs to be able to not only make the meal planner but have easy access to the recipe / video of how to make the foods on his diet to better help him continue on said diet.
  3. As a student he needs to be able to set up reminders and schedule his diet plan for times to make his meals and again like mentioned before the ability to save time figuriing out how to prepare said food to keep on his diet even with a busy students schedule.
  4. As a Person who is newly on a diet having a tracket that helps balance his exersize and diet is important so he doesnt have to constantly check calories and worry about his work outs it being pre planned and scheduled by him to stay within his goals and needs.
