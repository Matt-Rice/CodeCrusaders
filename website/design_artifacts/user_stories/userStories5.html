User Stories 5: Linda

Scenario 5: Diet

  1. As a registered nurse with type 2 diabetes, Linda wants an app that can track her daily calorie intake and provide meal suggestions so she can follow her diet plan and manage her blood sugar levels.
  2. As someone aiming to adopt a consistent fitness routine, Linda needs an app that allows her to schedule workouts so she can stay on track and follow her doctorâ€™s recommendations for physical activity.
  3. As a diabetic, Linda wants the app to help her identify foods she can and cannot eat so that she can make healthy choices that align with her dietary needs.
  4. As a busy professional, Linda wants the app to provide video links to tutorials for preparing diet-friendly meals so she can easily learn how to make meals that suit her dietary plan.
  5. As someone who has struggled to stick to diets in the past, Linda wants the app to send meal reminders based on her schedule so she can consistently follow her meal plan.
  6. As a health-conscious individual, Linda wants the app to track her progress toward her health goals, such as weight and blood sugar levels, so she can stay motivated and see the impact of her efforts.
  7. As a nurse with a busy schedule, Linda wants the app to allow her to customize meal plans based on her tastes and time constraints, making it easier for her to commit to her diet.
  8. As a fitness beginner, Linda wants access to tutorial videos linked to her scheduled workouts so she can confidently follow exercise routines tailored to her level and health needs.
