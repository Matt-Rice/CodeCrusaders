Persona 3: Bob

Bob, age 22, is a collage student at the university of louisville studying exersize science. he lives on campus in the dorms with a roommate. he has bought a gym membership this year and plans to make major changes to his diet and exersize. alot of his time is spend researching meal plans as well as exersizes to do to reach his goals.

bob wants to find one easy spot to plan his exersize sechedule as well as his meals for the week to stay within his fitness goals. he wants a way to track his calorie intake as well as his planned exersizes to stay within his goals.

bob struggles to keep track of everything going on in his life. his education with assignments classes and extercurricular actvivitys making time to go to the gym inbetween classes while keeping enough time for studying and homework as well as preparing meals that lie within his goals and price range.

bob is used to using his phone and health apps. but bob wants a health app that can actively remind him of his food plans and time for exersizing during his busy collage student schedule.
