Persona 5: Adam

Adam is a 30-year-old Advertising manager from Houston, Texas. Adam works at a big advertising firm and mostly spends his day in the office working with others, usually on work given by big companies such as Nike and PUMA. Having grown up in Houston, Adam attended the University of Houston and graduated with a business degree. He is experienced with technology, having used excel and many other programs during his work. He is currently married and lives with his wife in an apartment.

Recently, Adam has been feeling more stress over his job and is having trouble keeping things in order. Hoping to improve his health, Adam decided to track his health, this includes meal intake and sleeping patterns. Having a high demanding job, Adam would benefit from an app that is quick and easy to access, and be able to give him recommended meal plans based on what he needs to feel more healthy and energetic.
